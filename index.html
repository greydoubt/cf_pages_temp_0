Phrygian Snails

About Phrygian Snails

Phrygian snails are a species of land snail native to the Phrygian region of ancient Anatolia. These snails are known for their vibrant pink shells and their preference for damp, forested habitats.

Despite their small size, Phrygian snails play a crucial role in their ecosystem, contributing to nutrient cycling and serving as a food source for various predators.
